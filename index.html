The Origin of Doom

Victor Von Doom, ruler of Latveria and one of the most brilliant minds on Earth. After a failed experiment scarred his face, Victor created an advanced suit of armor and mask, becoming the feared Doctor Doom.

Powers & Abilities

Genius Intellect

One of the smartest individuals in the Marvel Universe, mastering multiple scientific disciplines.

Sorcery

Trained in the mystic arts, Doom wields powerful magic rivaling that of Doctor Strange.

Doom Armor

Advanced armor that grants superhuman strength, energy projection, and force fields.

Doombots

Robot duplicates that can stand in for Doom and execute his plans across the globe.

Latveria

The sovereign nation ruled by Doctor Doom with an iron fist. Though his methods are questionable, Latveria prospers under his rule with advanced technology and protection from outside threats.
